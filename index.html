Clubhouse Epsports

Welcome to the Howard Area Community Center Clubhouse Esports page. Our Esports team includes Super Smash Bros Ultimate, League Of Legends, NBA 2k, and Magic the Gathering. We practice from 3:30PM to 6:30PM on weekdays. We also practice on weekends.You are welcome to join. Bring your own controllers!

Super Smash Bros Ultimate

Super Samsh Bros Ultimate is fighting game up to 8 players based on your favorite characters from different Nitendo games.

League of Legends

League of Legends is a 5 player team based game where players are divided into 3 lanes and a jungle. The goal is for your team to destroy the enemy teams Nexus across the map.

NBA 2K19

NBA 2K19 is a basketball simulation based on the National Basketball Association.

Magic The Gathering Arena

Magic The Gathering Arena is the digital adaptation of Magic: The Gathering card game.
